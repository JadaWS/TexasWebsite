About Texas

Texas is the second-largest U.S. state by area and population. Known for its diverse landscapes and booming cities, Texas has a rich cultural heritage and independent spirit.

Quick Facts

  • Capital: Austin
  • Nickname: The Lone Star State
  • Statehood: December 29, 1845

    Top 3 Historical Facts About Texas

    1. Texas was an independent nation from 1836 to 1845 before joining the United States.
    2. The Battle of the Alamo in 1836 became a symbol of Texas’ fight for independence from Mexico.
    3. Texas is the only U.S. state to have had six different national flags fly over it, including Spain, France, and Mexico.
