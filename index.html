About Texas

Texas is the second-largest U.S. state by area and population. Known for its diverse landscapes and booming cities, Texas has a rich cultural heritage and independent spirit.

Quick Facts

  • Capital: Austin
  • Nickname: The Lone Star State
  • Statehood: December 29, 1845

Did You Know?

Texas was once an independent country — the Republic of Texas — from 1836 to 1845.
